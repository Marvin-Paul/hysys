**NOTE**

- The documents are deliberately interlocking, so the developer never has to guess.
- The 11 modules, 12 sectors, URL slugs, functional-requirement IDs, template names and brand colors are identical across all the five documents e.g.
- **The Product, Business & Functional Requirements** are traced into the **Technical, SEO & Analytics Specification**
- **The Site Map & Information Architecture** page inventory drives the **Content Plan & Page-Content Matrix** \- Content matrix
- The **Website Design Document templates** are what **the Content Plan & Page-Content Matrix** fills with copy.
- Documents 1, 2 and 3 would have been enough but Documents 4 and 5 are added because, in practice, a developer cannot ship a corporate site without the technical/SEO contract and the content plan - they are where most builds stall.
